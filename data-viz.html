Data Description

Our data is compiled from multiple reliable sources, in which we have taken information from each site to create accessible graphs that are easy to understand. Of the datasets that demonstrate the most frequently visited sites, it is a shared consensus that Google ranks as #1 among most visited websites, which can be further analyzed at Top Websites Ranking, United States Browsers Market Share, United States Most Used Search Engines in March 2023. For the most frequently visited social media sites, Social Media Stats United States of America lists Facebook as the #1 most visited social media platform, followed by Twitter and Pinterest. We chose this dataset because we live in a society where most of our lives involve the use of the internet or social media in some form or another, therefore, many people can relate to the data we’re seeing. There is a lot to analyze when observing which websites and platforms are the most widely used, and it’s interesting to dissect and try to understand why people prefer certain sites over others.
